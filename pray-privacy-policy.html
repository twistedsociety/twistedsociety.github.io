Pray – Daily Prayer & Journal

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2025  ·  Twisted Society AB

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Twisted Society AB collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Pray – Daily Prayer & Journal.

By using Pray, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to operate and improve Pray:

Category Examples Purpose
User Content Journal entries, personal prayers, chat messages, voice input text Deliver the core journaling and prayer features
Preferences Language selection, Bible version, voice choice, notification settings Personalise your experience
Usage Data Feature interactions, session frequency, subscription status Improve app performance and prioritise features
Crash & Error Data Crash logs, error reports (anonymised) Diagnose and fix technical issues
Device Information Device type, OS version, locale Ensure compatibility and localisation

We do not collect your name, email address, or any information that directly identifies you, unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, when contacting support).

2 How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • provide, operate, and maintain the app and its features
  • generate personalised prayers and responses using AI
  • sync your data securely across your devices (via Firebase)
  • send daily prayer reminders via push notifications (with your permission)
  • analyse anonymised usage trends to improve the app
  • diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
  • process subscription purchases (handled entirely by Apple)

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3 AI Features & Data Processing

When you use AI-powered features — such as generating a prayer, using the chat assistant, or converting voice to text — the content of your request is sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI processes this data under their own privacy policy and does not use API data to train their models by default.

Avoid including sensitive personal information (e.g. full names, financial details, medical records) in prayers or chat messages.

OpenAI Privacy Policy: openai.com/privacy

4 Third-Party Services

OpenAI AI prayer generation, chat, and voice transcription openai.com/privacy
Google Firebase (Realtime Database) Secure cloud storage for prayers, journals, and preferences firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Firebase Crashlytics Anonymised crash reporting and error tracking firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which employs industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We implement security measures appropriate for the sensitivity of your data.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Jailbreaking or rooting your device removes OS-level security protections and may put your stored data at risk. We strongly advise against it.

6 Data Retention

We retain your User Content and preferences for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Anonymised analytics and crash data may be retained longer for product improvement purposes.

If you would like your data deleted, please contact us at twistedsocietyab@gmail.com and we will remove your data within a reasonable timeframe.

8 Children's Privacy

Pray is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9 International Data Transfers

Pray is operated from Sweden and your data may be processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including the United States (via Firebase and OpenAI). When we transfer data outside the EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses as required by applicable law.
